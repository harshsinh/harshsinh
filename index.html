Robocon'16

Robocon is a robotics competetion organised by ABU where team from across Asia-Pacific take part. This years problem statement requires us to build a steering robot without a driving mechanism capable of line-following and another robot that drives the steering robot without contact. This problem statement also includes wall-following, line-following and pole-climbing.

AUV

Autonomus Underwater Vehicle is an international competetion organised by AUVSI foundation and in part by USNAV. This competetion aims at building ........ well AUVs. Very few teams are able to participate in it due to the harsh condition and difficulty in automation inside water. It pretty much tries to incorporate a lot form robotics such as Line following, Image processing, Trajectory tracing etcetra but mind you all of this is to be done Underwater!
